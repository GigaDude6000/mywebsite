Why I Make Music

Music is something I like since being a child. I remember downloading some of my favorite songs from YouTube to put on my iPod. My father and I built a DIY DJ set where I could play and fade music with a mixing console. So as a kid I loved MUSIC.

My favorite artists are DVRST and Avicii. My own musical name is Norret.

Aristotle criticized certain types of music and instruments, such as the flute, for being overly exciting and not contributing to the mind's intellectual development. He argued that playing the flute "contributes nothing to the mind" and that music aimed merely at giving pleasure to a vulgar audience leads to the vulgarization of both performers and listeners, effectively shutting off thoughtful engagement.

I love IDM, creating a deeper layer in my music.
